The Moka Spot

The Moka Spot was established in 1997. We decided to open this coffee shop because couldn't find a place that allowed you to enjoy a weekend morning out, that just served simple espresso drinks, made in a Moka pot and homestyle breakfasts the way your mom used to make it at home on a Sunday morning. The Moka Spot is a place where you can stop in and get your morning coffee, sit a while and read your favorite newspaper or book, or visit with your family and friends and enjoy a casual brunch.

We offer organic sustainable coffees and teas, and source our produce and other groceries as locally as we can. We are your neighborhood coffee shop, but more than that we are your neighbor and your friend. Stop by for a visit. You'll be glad you did, we promise.
